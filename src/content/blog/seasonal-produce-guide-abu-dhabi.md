Shopping for seasonal produce is one of the simplest ways to improve your meals while saving money. Seasonal fruits and vegetables are harvested at peak ripeness, travel shorter distances, and require less storage time, all of which translates to better flavor, higher nutritional value, and lower cost.

## Summer Fruits in Season

Abu Dhabi's summer months bring an incredible variety of juicy, refreshing fruits perfect for beating the heat. Mangoes are at their peak right now, with varieties from India, Pakistan, and Egypt offering sweet, aromatic flavors that are ideal for smoothies, salads, or eating fresh.

Watermelon and melon varieties are also in abundance during summer. Look for watermelons with a creamy yellow spot on the underside, which indicates they ripened in the sun. Melons should feel heavy for their size and emit a sweet fragrance at the stem end.

Grapes, both red and green varieties, arrive fresh from regional vineyards and imported sources. Figs are another summer specialty that pair beautifully with cheese and honey for a quick, elegant dessert.

## Summer Vegetables at Their Best

The hot months are excellent for tomatoes, eggplants, peppers, and cucumbers. These heat-loving vegetables develop intense flavors and firm textures when grown in warm conditions. Our [fresh vegetables](/services/fresh-vegetables/) section is stocked daily with the best summer selections.

Okra, a staple in Arabic and Indian cooking, is also at its peak during summer months. Look for small, tender pods that snap cleanly when bent. Larger okra pods tend to be fibrous and tough.

Zucchini and summer squash are versatile vegetables that grill beautifully, roast to caramelized perfection, or blend into soups and sauces. We carry both local and imported varieties to suit every recipe and budget.

## Leafy Greens and Herbs

While summer heat can be hard on delicate greens, we source our [leafy greens](/services/leafy-greens/) and [fresh herbs](/services/fresh-herbs/) from climate-controlled farms and suppliers to ensure they arrive crisp and vibrant. Spinach, lettuce, rocket, and watercress are excellent choices for light summer salads.

Our herb selection includes coriander, mint, parsley, dill, basil, and curry leaves. Summer is the perfect time to experiment with fresh herb pestos, infused waters, and herb-heavy marinades for grilled meats and vegetables.

## Why Seasonal Matters

Seasonal produce offers three key advantages. First, flavor: fruits and vegetables harvested in season have had time to develop their natural sugars and complex flavors. Second, nutrition: produce picked at peak ripeness contains higher levels of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. Third, value: abundant seasonal supply means lower prices for the same quality.
